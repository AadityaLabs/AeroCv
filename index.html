Why Aaditya Kandpal started AeroCV

AeroCV was born out of frustration with current online cv creation engines. Most builders charge heavy premiums, overlay heavy non-removable watermarks, and offer rigid single-column structures.

Our platform is different. It provides real-time, zero-lag dynamic custom previews, matched cover letter alignments, premium light and dark themes, and completely unified job pipeline analytics on a secure local platform.
